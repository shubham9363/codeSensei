1.1 PURPOSE
The primary purpose of this comprehensive software engineering project report is to provide an in-depth, exhaustive, and fully documented analysis of the CodeSensei application. CodeSensei is conceived as a state-of-the-art interactive coding education platform with gamified learning mechanisms designed to revolutionize how students approach algorithmic problem-solving. This document is intended to serve as the ultimate blueprint, covering every single nuance of the software development life cycle (SDLC). It aims to provide stakeholders, developers, project managers, and quality assurance engineers with an unambiguous, granular description of what CodeSensei is, how it operates internally, the management strategies implemented to deliver it on time, the architectural design that ensures its scalability, and the rigorous testing regimens employed to guarantee its reliability.
